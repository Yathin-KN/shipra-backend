Here are a few of Our World-Class Projects

Project: Statue of Unity, World’s largest statue built  in Gujarat, India

World’s largest statue of Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, which was constructed in 2018, with 21,0000 m3 of concrete, 18,500 tonnes of reinforcement, 6500 tonnes of structural steel, and 1800 tonnes of bronze.

Challenge

After the concrete and steelwork, bronze cladded panels were to be connected to the truss frame to support the weight of the cladding system and resist earthquake and wind forces. Also, the clad panels were designed to have overlapping panels that allow the vertical and horizontal movements of panels.
The challenge was to create and provide effective sealants that could hold the panels together and give a seamless look.

Our Solutions

Looking at the requirement, we suggested high-performing silicone sealants and the application technique best suited for holding the panels effectively and strongly even against weather forces or disasters like earthquakes or storms.
The further challenge was to create a seamless look by making the sealant color match the bronze color of the panels.
Sealants generally are available in white, black, or grey shades.
Maintaining the quality of the sealant we devised a perfect combination to create the bronze shade that matched the color of the panel.

Our innovative approach and meticulous work earned us the complete project of not only sealants but also tile adhesives, water-repellent, and the testing and checking of the application of chemicals and the workmanship involved.

Project: Surat Diamond Bourse, World’s Largest Office Building in Gujarat, India

The Surat Diamond Bourse, as the complex is called, has a floor space of 660,000 sq m compared with the 600,000 sq m for the US armed forces’ celebrated headquarters.
The whole complex consists of nine interconnected 15-story towers containing 4,200 offices, serviced by 131 lifts, with speeds of 3m/s, and are surrounded by amenities such as conference halls, meeting spaces, restaurants, banks, and shops. There is also a customs house with a national diamond research institute, an international convention center, international education facilities, and five-star hotels.

Challenge

Procuring and delivering the right solution needed for a project of this size and volume was one of the biggest challenges we have dealt with. Testing the application quality and performance of the solution provided by us was another huge and critical task on our hands.

Our Solutions

The requirement was to get the construction chemicals for the Glass and Stone Facade of all the wings (buildings).We followed a very meticulous approach as we went ahead with assessing the need, determining the quantity, procuring and delivering the right quantity, and right quality at the right time.
The pandemic phase made it more challenging yet we succeeded in managing our timelines.
Additionally, we carried out testing for sealants application to ensure that the sealants provided by us are applied well for optimum performance.
Our team conducted detailed on-site inspections for 6 months to ensure the best results.
